Modelling in Inventor
May 2017, Introduction to Engineering and Design
This was an independent project I completed. The purpose of this project was to create a 3D model of a stationary object and get practice working with 3D modelling software. The hardest part of this project was recreating the tiny, barely visible details of the lead case, such as the embossed letters or the tiny indentations in the bottom of the case. Overall I learned how to use various features in the software and that patience and accuracy are key to creating a good model.

Scratch Project
September 2017, Computer Science and Engineering
The objective of this project was to create a game using Scratch. I took turns coding with my partner, helped research certain ideas to incorporate in our game, and helped to document information in and complete our design notebook. Fixing the glitches and perfecting the gravity in our game were the toughest and most tedious challenges to overcome. This project gave me a better understanding of programming in scratch.

App Inventor Project
September 2017- October 2017, Computer Science and Engineering
The objective of this project was to create a functional app using MIT App Inventor. I took turns coding with my partner, helped research certain ideas to incorporate in our game, and helped to document information in and complete our design notebook. The toughest part of our project was figuring out how to incorporate pictures into our app. In the end we realized that we had not downloaded all of our images to app inventor, which led to them not appearing in our app. I learned to pay attention to directions and review every tiny detail of our code in order to prevent any bugs.

Atmotu.0
June 2016, Camp Bizsmart
The objective of this project was to create a new and improved version of the already existing product known as Atmotube. As Chief Voice of the Customer(CVOC) in a company of seven, my role was to identify our target consumers, compare our product to our competition, identify consumer preferences, and gauge consumer responses to our product. I learned a lot about the inner workings of a company and the importance of communication and teamwork in a large group.
