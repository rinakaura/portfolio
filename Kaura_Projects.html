Python Image Artist

I took turns coding with my partner, helped collect images to incorporate in our project, and helped to document information in and complete our design notebook. Python Hangman
January 2018- February 2018, Computer Science and Engineering
The objective of this project was to create a themed hangman game using python. I coded with my partner, researched certain features to incorporate in our game, and helped to document information in and complete our design notebook. The toughest part of our project was figuring out how to substitue the letters for the blank spaces in hangman without causing an error. In the end, we rewrote our code from scratch with just loops and such methods, then grouped the code into different function definitions. I learned that sometimes you must seek unconventional ways to solve a problem, and that just because you were taught one way, does not mean that way will always produce the desired results.
